# Healthcare Analytics Dashboard (Power BI)

## Project Overview

This project presents a Healthcare Analytics Dashboard built using Power
BI to analyze hospital operations and financial performance. The
dashboard converts raw healthcare data into meaningful insights to
understand patient visits, department workload, insurance coverage,
payment status, and treatment costs.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

## Dataset Description

The dataset simulates real-world hospital operational data and includes
the following tables:

-   patients -- Patient demographic information
-   visits -- Patient visit details including service type and treatment
    cost
-   departments -- Hospital departments such as Cardiology, Neurology,
    Orthopedics
-   providers -- Healthcare providers involved in treatments
-   diagnoses -- Medical diagnosis data
-   procedures -- Treatment procedures performed
-   insurance -- Insurance provider information
-   cities -- Patient location data

These tables are connected through relationships to represent a
healthcare data model.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

## Dashboard Structure

### Page 1 -- Financial Overview

Key KPIs: - Total Visits - Total Treatment Cost - Insurance Coverage -
Total Medication Cost - Total Room Charges

Visualizations: - Revenue by Department - Hospital Visits Trend
(Monthly) - Insurance Coverage by Provider

Purpose: To analyze the financial performance of hospital services and
insurance contributions.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

### Page 2 -- Operational Insights

Visualizations: - Patient Visits by Department - Hospital Visits by
Service Type - Patient Visits by Insurance Provider - Payment Status
Distribution - Average Treatment Cost by Department - Monthly Patient
Visit Trend

Purpose: To understand department workload, service distribution, and
patient demand trends.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

## Key Insights

-   Cardiology and General Surgery manage the highest patient visits.
-   Outpatient services account for the majority of hospital visits.
-   Insurance providers cover a significant portion of treatment costs.
-   A percentage of payments remain pending.
-   Monthly trends help hospitals anticipate patient demand.
